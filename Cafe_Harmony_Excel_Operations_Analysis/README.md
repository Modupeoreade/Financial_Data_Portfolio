# Café Harmony Excel Operations Analysis

This project analyzes Café Harmony’s operational efficiency, customer behaviour, product performance, and staff performance using Microsoft Excel. It highlights key business challenges and provides data‑driven recommendations to improve revenue, customer satisfaction, and inventory management.

---

## 📊 Business Problem

Café Harmony has grown rapidly and now faces several operational challenges:

- Identifying top‑performing menu items across locations  
- Understanding customer profiles and spending behaviour  
- Improving stock management to reduce stockouts and overstock  
- Detecting underperforming locations or staff issues  

This Excel‑based analysis provides insights to support strategic decision‑making.

## 📌 Key Performance Indicators

- **£12,590 Total Revenue** – modest performance  
- **3.047 Average Rating** – indicates service or product issues  
- **1,003 Customers** – strong footfall but low average spend (£12.59)  
- **10 Stock Items** – high risk of stockouts  
- **1,000 Employees** – efficiency concerns  

Overall: **Revenue is low relative to staff size and customer volume.**

---

## 🧁 Product Performance Insights

- **Muffins (£2,401)** – highest revenue item  
- **Iced Tea (£2,235)** – strong performer  
- **All items** show consistent demand  
- **Cappuccino** slightly underperforms  
- High‑spending customers prefer **snack + drink combinations**

**Recommendation:** Increase stock for muffins and sandwiches; analyse upselling opportunities by staff and location.

## 📅 Monthly Sales Trends

- **April & September** – highest sales  
- **May & August** – noticeable declines  
- Summer months show stable demand  

**Insight:**  
Align staffing and inventory with peak months; investigate reasons for off‑peak drops.

---

## ⭐ Customer Ratings

- Most ratings are **3‑star (206)** and **4‑star (217)**  
- **1‑star (190)** and **2‑star (188)** indicate dissatisfaction  
- **5‑star (199)** shows potential for improvement  

**Insight:**  
Service consistency and stock availability need improvement.

---

## 👤 Customer Demographics

- **18–27 age group (25%)** – strong demand for quick, affordable items  
- **28–37 & 58–67** – significant contributors  
- Younger customers drive snack and drink sales  

**Insight:**  
Target promotions and stock planning around younger shoppers during peak months.

---

## 📦 Inventory Performance

Negative stock performance:

- Milk  
- Iced tea bags  
- Croissant dough  
- Muffin mix  

Better‑controlled items:

- Sandwich bread  
- Sugar  

**Insight:**  
Stockouts are affecting customer satisfaction and ratings.

---

## 🧾 Summary of Findings

- Total sales: **£12,590**  
- Top products: **Muffins & Iced Tea**  
- Peak months: **April & September**  
- Largest customer segment: **38–47 age group**  
- Average rating: **3.047**  
- Staff performance varies significantly  
- Stock issues impact customer experience  

---

## 📌 Recommendations

- **Improve inventory management** using demand forecasting  
- **Target key age groups** (38–47 and younger shoppers)  
- **Enhance customer experience** through staff training and stock reliability  
- **Optimize staff efficiency** by deploying top performers during peak hours  
- **Align operations with seasonal trends**  
- **Boost customer loyalty** with improved rewards programs
