# TRENDMART Retail Profitability & Margin Optimisation Analytics

## 📌 Project Overview  
This project analyses TRENDMART’s retail performance to uncover margin leakage, identify unprofitable product lines, and recommend data‑driven strategies to improve profitability.  
The analysis combines **Power BI**, **DAX**, and **business analytics** to deliver executive‑level insights for commercial decision‑making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives  
- Analyse revenue, cost, and margin performance across product categories  
- Identify high‑margin vs low‑margin segments  
- Detect operational inefficiencies affecting profitability  
- Provide actionable recommendations to improve overall margin performance  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Demonstrated  
- **Power BI** (Data modelling, DAX, interactive dashboards)  
- **Data Cleaning & Transformation** (Power Query)  
- **Financial Analysis** (COGS, margin %, profitability segmentation)  
- **Business Insight Storytelling**  
- **Executive Reporting & Visualisation**  

---

## 📊 Key Insights  
- **Kidswear** contributed the highest revenue but showed margin compression due to rising COGS.  
- **Menswear** delivered strong margins but low volume — signalling an opportunity for targeted marketing.  
- **Accessories** showed the highest margin % but inconsistent sales patterns.  
- Operational inefficiencies were identified in **inventory turnover** and **discounting strategy**.  

---

## 💡 Recommendations  
- **Reprice low‑margin SKUs** to recover lost margin.  
- **Increase marketing investment** in high‑margin, low‑volume categories.  
- **Optimise discounting rules** to reduce unnecessary margin erosion.  
- **Improve inventory planning** to stabilise stock levels and reduce stockouts.
